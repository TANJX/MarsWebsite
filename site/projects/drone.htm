Documentary: Make difference

2016-8-9

People see things every day. Our eyes transfer the light signals to our brain and generate images. This builds our fundamental cognition of this world- the routine one with familiar scenes. But what about breaking out of these routines? What the world will be like while seeing it from different ways?

I believe Drone piloting offers a satisfying answer to it by providing a completely different perspective of observing.
