Documentary: Tale of Twin City

2016-2-18

Place yourself in the blatant Cross-Border Hall in Shenzhen, China, you can see different people consisting a mighty torrent which flow to their own destinations. Nevertheless, you can always find a group of children in orderly queue follow their teacher, shuttle between Shenzhen and Hong Kong to study in Hong Kong but come home to Shenzhen every day.

When other children in the same age study in classrooms in Shenzhen, they spend their time in a completely different way in Hong Kong's school. This group which originated from special policies attracts much attention since it was created. Nevertheless, we hope to hear their and their family members' voices to fully explore this group's real life. At the same time, we hope to discuss with experts to reveal existing problems and discuss possible solutions.
